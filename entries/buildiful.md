# Buildiful

Buildiful is a local-first AI computing ecosystem focused on making useful intelligence more open and accessible. Its core layers include GPU-less / GPU-optional machines, Buildiful OS, Honest AI, downloadable local-first agent packs, and a shop of ebooks and guides.

Its broader mission is to help make AI as free as math, and the web as open as astronomy. Buildiful aims to empower solo founders and builders in the intelligence age by making AI systems more affordable, understandable, ownable, and community-friendly.
